The Belmont Legacy

From Simon to Richter, the Belmont clan has battled Dracula for centuries. Their tale is marked by sacrifice, bravery, and a fateful curse.

  • Simon Belmont – The original vampire hunter
  • Alucard – Dracula’s son turned savior
  • Richter Belmont – Hero of Symphony of the Night
